# Reserved Words
ShortHand has few reserved words that cannot be used to name variables or functions.  
Below is the list of all such words:  

* * *
` |  |  |   
---|---|---|---  
`ALL` | `AND` | `AS` | `BREAK`  
`CONTINUE` | `ELSE` | `ELSEIF` | `ELSIF`  
`END` | `FOR` | `FOREACH` | `FUNCTION`  
`GRID` | `IF` | `INCLUDE` | `JUMP`  
`LOCAL` | `NEW` | `NOT` | `NULL`  
`OR` | `PRINT` | `PRINTLN` | `RETURN`  
`THEN` | `TO` | `WHILE` | `STRING`  
`INT` | `FLOAT` | `CSPLIT` |   
`
